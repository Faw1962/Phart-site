About Phart

Visionary Leadership in Music Production

Mohamed Amine Jarraya

Founder & Creative Director

Phart represents the fusion of artistic vision and technical excellence in music event production. Founded by Mohamed Amine Jarraya, a passionate connoisseur of musical culture and technical expert in audio and video production, we specialize in creating large-scale, memorable concerts and innovative music experiences that resonate with audiences worldwide.

Our strategic approach combines creativity with expertise in complex project management and team leadership, ensuring every production exceeds expectations and creates lasting emotional connections with audiences. We believe in the transformative power of music to create unforgettable moments.
